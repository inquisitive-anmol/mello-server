Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to Mello! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the Application includes:

  • Personal Data: Personally identifiable information, such as your phone number, name, and profile picture, which you voluntarily give to us when you register.
  • Audio and Video Data: We require microphone and camera permissions to facilitate real-time voice and video calls between users through WebRTC/LiveKit. We do not record or store your private conversations unless explicitly stated and consented to for safety features.
  • Financial Data: We may collect data related to your payment method (e.g. valid credit card number, card brand, expiration date) through our payment processor (Razorpay) when you add funds to your wallet. We store very limited, if any, financial information that we collect.

2. How We Use Your Information

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the Application to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Process your payments and manage your wallet balance.
  • Enable real-time communication (voice/video calls) with other users.
  • Match you with other users based on your selected "Vibes".
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience with the Application.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share information we have collected about you in certain situations. Your information may be disclosed as follows:

  • By Law or to Protect Rights: If we believe the release of information about you is necessary to respond to legal process, to investigate or remedy potential violations of our policies, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of others.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with third parties that perform services for us or on our behalf, including payment processing (Razorpay), SMS verification (Fast2SMS), and real-time communication infrastructure (LiveKit).

4. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@mello.app.
