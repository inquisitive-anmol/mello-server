Account Deletion Instructions

If you would like to permanently delete your Mello account and all associated data, you can do so directly within the mobile application.

  1. Open the Mello app and log in to your account.
  2. Navigate to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap on the Settings icon (or Settings menu).
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Note: Deleting your account is irreversible. All your data, wallet balance, and chat history will be permanently erased.
If you have lost access to your device or need administrative assistance deleting your account, please contact us at support@mello.com.
